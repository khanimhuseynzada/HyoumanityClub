What is hYOUmanity? hYOUmanity is a place of love, hope, care, inspiration and dreams coming true. Our inspirational quote is " hYOUmanity starts with You" - you are the stimulator of our activities. Our aims are to nurture and bring people together.

Our projects

KEEPWARM

A blanket and hot tea are a great way to keep yourself warm. There is also another way to KEEP WARM - by writing letters full of emotions to our retirement house residents. We all know the feeling of warmth when someone shares their positive energy with us, makes us feel wanted and loved. You can support our project by gifting tea, candies, homemade cake and cupcakes!
