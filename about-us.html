Hello, I'm Kind!

Hyoumanity club is a channel through which people can help other people, animals and nature through charity projects. hYOUmanity is a place of love, hope, care, inspiration and dreams coming true. Our inspirational quote is hYOUmanity starts with You" - you are the stimulator of our activities. Our aims are to nurture and bring people together.
